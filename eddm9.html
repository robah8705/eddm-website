Benefits of the 9X12 Postcard

👉 Cost-Effective Marketing – You share the mailing cost with 13-15 other businesses, making this way more affordable than a solo direct mail campaign.

👉 Exclusive Business Category – You're the only business of your type featured. No competing ads on the same postcard!

👉 Huge, Eye-Catching Format – Unlike regular-sized mailers that get lost in the shuffle, this 9x12 postcard stands out and demands attention.

👉 No Mailing List Needed – We use EDDM to deliver to upscale 5,000 plus homes in the Licking County area, ensuring maximum exposure.

👉 Tangible & Trusted – People trust direct mail more than digital ads. They hold onto it, stick it on the fridge, and refer back to it when they need services.

👉 Higher ROI – Because the mailing is hyper-local, you're reaching people who are most likely to buy from you.

👉 Bonus: Built-in Offers – You can include discounts, QR codes, or special promotions to drive action immediately!
